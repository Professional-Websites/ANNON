What is the tax for token purchase and what it is used for?

Buy/Sell tax is 5%. 3% of tax goes to development of the Anonymous Network and 2% go to marketing

Will Anonymous Network sell user data to advertisers?

We do not have access to identifiable user data. The anonymized aggregated data used for accounting and reporting cannot be mapped back to devices or user identities of any kind

Are all ads blocked or can users allow some or all?

Tracking scripts (trackers) and ads that depend on them are blocked by default. You can allow ads and trackers in the Shields settings globally and for each site.

How do you relieve concerns that you are spyware?

We use all-open source, and we welcome help in auditing our source and verifying our binaries on Debian Linux (verified binaries provably derive from a given version of open source).

Why build a browser and not extensions for top browsers?

Extensions face API and performance limits. Additionally, popular extension stores often host malicious counterfeit extensions, which have lead to millions of infected users. Building our own browser lets us put our best foot forward on matters of speed and privacy.

Why doesn’t Anonymous Network block ads on search engine result pages?

While we will block third-party cookies where you have no first-party relationship with the cookie’s domain, we don’t block first party cookies by default. We will have the option to selectively block/enable cookies globally or on a site-by-site basis.

What is Anonymous Network Wallet and $ANNON Rewards?

By logging into your Anonymous Network Wallet or Metamask account, you can exchange or transfer cryptocurrencies. You will also be able to stake $ANNON tokens for aditional premium services. You wallet allows you to Earn and Give anonymously. With $ANNON rewards, you can enable Ads to earn anonymously and get paid for your attention.

What happens if I have money in my Anonymous Network wallet and my computer crashes or is stolen or lost?

Your Anonymous Network wallet comes with a set of anonymized recovery codes. You can copy, print, and/or file your anonymized recovery codes and create a backup. It is important to note that it is your sole responsibility to store these codes in a safe and secure place because nobody else is able to recover the funds in your wallet if you lose these codes.

If you install Anonymous Network on two different computers, do you get two unique personas for the Rewards system, or one?

You get multiple personas and you can synchronise them securely and anonymously across all of your devices.

Are my contributions and tips really anonymous?

All automatic contributions are anonymized such that no one can link these transactions with a specific user’s wallet or browsing activity, thanks to the extensive use of privacy preserving cryptographic protocols.
